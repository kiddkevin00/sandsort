Features

What's in the build

The whole feature set, plainly listed — no upsells gating the basics.

⇣

Tap, tap, pour

Tap a jar to lift it, tap another to pour. The whole top run of the source color transfers at once.

◖

8 sequential levels

3 colors → 8 colors, 1–2 spare jars. Levels unlock as you clear them.

↶

Full undo

Move history is preserved; Undo walks back one pour at a time. No 'watch ad to undo'.

⤳

Deterministic deals

Every deal is generated from a Mulberry32 seed; the same seed reproduces the same board. New deal reshuffles within the level.

⊞

No trivial openings

Generator reshuffles to avoid starting with a jar that's already one color. Every level needs at least a few pours to feel a level.

◷

Best moves saved

Per-level best move count is tracked. Fewer is better.

How to play

From cold-start to good-at-it

A few short steps. The app gets out of your way after that.

01

Tap a jar

It lifts to show it's selected.

02

Tap another jar

If the destination is empty or its top matches the source's top color, the top run pours.

03

Sort everything

Goal: every jar is one color (or empty). When the level clears, the next unlocks.

04

Use Undo freely

Made a wrong call? Undo walks back. Restart resets the same deal. New deal reshuffles.
