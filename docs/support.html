Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

Is this a clone of something?

No. The pour-and-sort mechanic (Water Sort, Ball Sort, Sand Sort) is a long-running puzzle pattern with many independent implementations. Sandsort is an original take with its own UI, palette, code, and branding.

What if I'm truly stuck?

Tap Undo to roll back, or Restart to reset the deal, or New deal to reshuffle within the level.

Can I jump back to easier levels?

Yes — the menu shows all unlocked levels and your best move count for each one.

Why no timer?

Because timing-pressuring puzzle games is a design choice that doesn't match this app's quiet positioning.

Get in touch

Need help, or want to report a bug?

Open an issue on GitHub. There's no support email — issues are the canonical channel and they're public, which keeps everything honest.
